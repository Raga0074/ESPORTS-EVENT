2.Call of Duty

Rules & Regulations:

1. Team Composition: Teams should consist of 4-6 players. Each team must designate their captain who will be responsible for the team.
2. Tournament Format: The Tournament will follow a double-elimination bracket Format. maps and modes will be predetermined and communicated to teams before each match.
3. Match Settings:
 a)Game Mode: Typically, Search and Destroy, Hardpoint, Domination, and Control modes will be used.
 b)Time Limit: Standard time limit for each round.
 c)Score Limit: Standard score limit for each mode.
 d)Killstreaks: Allowed, but restricted to a specific list determined by the tournament organizers.
 e)Loadout Restrictions: Any banned weapons, attachments, perks, or equipment will be communicated before the tournament.
 f)Killcam: On.
 g)CODCasting: Enabled for tournament organizers and casters.
4. Prize Distribution: Please note that prizes will be announced on the day of the tournaments.
5. Schedule: The schedule will be disclosed on the day of the tournament based on the number of registrations recorded.

3.BGMI

Rules & Regulations:

1. Eligibility: The tournament is open to all current students of the college.
  Participants must have a valid Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) account to compete.
2. Registration: Teams must register their participation in advance via an online registration form provided by the organizing committee.
  Each team must consist of exactly four members.
3. Team Composition: Teams must maintain the same roster throughout the tournament. No substitutions are allowed once the tournament commences.
4. Format:
 a)The tournament will be conducted in a knockout format.
 b)Matches will be played in squads mode.
5. Schedule: A detailed schedule including match timings, dates, and rounds will be provided to all registered teams before the start of the tournament.
6. Fair Play: Cheating of any kind, including but not limited to hacking, exploiting bugs, or using unauthorized third-party software,
  will result in immediate disqualification of the offending team.
7. Scoring: Points will be awarded based on performance in each match, including kills and final placement.

4.e-football League

Rules & Regulations:

1. Eligibility:
 a. Participants must be at least 16 years old to compete.
 b. Participants must have a valid eFootball Mobile account.
 c. Participants must adhere to the terms and conditions set forth by the tournament organizers.
2. Registration:
 a. Registration is required to participate in the tournament.
 b. Registration deadlines will be announced before the start of the tournament.
3. Format:
 a. The tournament will follow a knockout or group stage format, depending on the number of participants.
 b. Matches will be played in a 1v1 format.
4. Scheduling:
 a. Match schedules will be communicated to participants before the start of the tournament.
 b. Participants must be available to play matches at the scheduled times.
 c. Failure to show up for a match without prior notification may result in disqualification.
5. Match Reporting:
 a. Participants are responsible for reporting match results to tournament organizers.
 b. Screenshots or videos may be required as proof of match results.
6. Prizes:
 a. Prizes will be awarded to winners and possibly runners-up, depending on the tournament structure and sponsorship.
 b. Prize distribution will be communicated by tournament organizers.
7. Rule Modifications:
 a. Tournament organizers reserve the right to modify or update the rules as necessary.
 b. Participants will be notified of any rule changes in advance.
